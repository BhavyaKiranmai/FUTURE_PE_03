# FUTURE_PE_03
AI SEO BLOG &amp; CONTENT CLUSTER GENERATOR FOR BUSINESS WEBSITES

### Business Chosen

SmileCare Dental Clinic – Bangalore

### Target Audience

* Patients seeking dental treatment
* Families searching for dental care
* Working professionals
* Individuals looking for cosmetic dentistry services

### Project Objective

To design a structured prompt engineering system that generates SEO-optimized blogs and content clusters for dental clinic websites.

### Problem Statement

Many dental clinics struggle to rank on Google due to unstructured content, lack of keyword targeting, and poor local SEO strategies. This project demonstrates how AI and prompt engineering can be used to create effective SEO content systems.

### Prompt Engineering Approach

* Keyword and search intent targeting
* SEO-friendly content structure
* Content cluster generation
* Local SEO optimization
* Internal linking opportunities

### Content Cluster Strategy

#### Pillar Blog

* Best Dental Clinic in Bangalore – Services, Cost & What to Expect

#### Supporting Blogs

* Dental Implant Cost in Bangalore
* Teeth Whitening – Is It Safe and Effective?
* How to Choose the Right Dentist in Bangalore
* Root Canal Treatment: Procedure, Benefits and Cost

### Deliverables

* SEO Outline
* Keyword Strategy
* 1 Pillar Blog
* 4 Supporting Blogs
* Prompt Templates
* Local SEO Strategy

### Conclusion

This project demonstrates how Prompt Engineering, AI, and SEO strategies can be combined to create ranking-focused content systems for local businesses. The workflow can be adapted for clinics, agencies, coaching institutes, salons, and other service-based businesses.
