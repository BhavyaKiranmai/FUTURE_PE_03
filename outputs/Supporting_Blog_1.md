## Introduction

Dental implants are one of the most effective solutions for replacing missing teeth. They look natural, improve chewing ability, and help maintain jawbone health. Many patients in Bangalore choose dental implants because of their durability and long-term benefits.

## What Are Dental Implants?

Dental implants are artificial tooth roots made of titanium that are placed into the jawbone. A crown is then attached to provide a natural-looking replacement tooth.

## Factors Affecting Dental Implant Cost

### Type of Implant

Different implant brands and materials can affect the overall cost.

### Number of Implants

The total expense depends on how many teeth need replacement.

### Additional Procedures

Bone grafting or tooth extraction may increase the treatment cost.

### Dentist's Experience

Highly experienced specialists may charge more for advanced expertise.

## FAQs

### Are dental implants permanent?
Dental implants are designed to last many years with proper care.

### Is the procedure painful?
The procedure is usually performed under local anesthesia and is generally comfortable.

### How long does treatment take?
It may take several months depending on healing requirements.
